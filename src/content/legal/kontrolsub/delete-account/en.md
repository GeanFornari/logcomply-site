# Account Deletion — KontrolSub

To permanently delete your **KontrolSub** account, please follow the procedures below:

---

## Request Procedure

To ensure the security and ownership of the account, deletion must be formally requested by email:

1.  Send an email to: **contato@vektordigital.com.br**
2.  Email subject: **Account Deletion Request - KontrolSub**
3.  In the body of the message, you must provide the **registered email address** of the account you wish to remove.
4.  *(Optional)* If you wish, briefly describe the reason for deletion. Your feedback is valuable for us to improve our service.

---

## What happens after the request?

Once the request is received and the account owner's identity is confirmed:

*   **Complete Removal:** All your registered personal data (name, email, and UID) will be permanently removed.
*   **Subscription Data:** All registered subscriptions, reminders, and spending histories will be deleted from our servers.
*   **Irreversibility:** This process is definitive and **irreversible**. After completion, it will not be possible to recover any information or account history.

The deletion will be processed within a reasonable period, in accordance with current data protection regulations (**GDPR**).

---

## Questions?
If you have any questions about this process, please contact us through the same support email mentioned above.
